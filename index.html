About Hazelnart

Two Decades of
Creative Excellence

Founded with a burning passion for impactful design, Hazelnart has stood at the forefront of Ghana's graphic design and printing industry for over 20 years. We don't just create designs — we build brands that command attention and drive results.

From intimate business branding to large-scale corporate identities and international organisations, our portfolio speaks quality, creativity, and precision. Every project reflects our commitment to work that truly resonates.

Based in the heart of Accra Newtown, we serve clients across Ghana and beyond — delivering world-class design solutions tailored to every unique vision.
